Artificial Intelligence Technologies and Applications in Computer Vision.

Textbooks?

These lectures are hopefully reasonably "self-sufficient" - I've tried to make everything essentially start from quite elementary. Likewise, I've tried to make the contents useful/understandable without the technical detail - for examples of actual compuyter vision models people fit to data. I truly expect that with perhaps your favourite search engine/online "goto" resources (mine happen to be Google and Arxiv and Wikepedia and Youtube and Simons Institute....) you can both fill in any gaps in my lecture slides or find background or advanced material.

That said - and especially since I was asked for a recommendation for a good *Computer Vision* book, I was prompted to put a few slides together...
